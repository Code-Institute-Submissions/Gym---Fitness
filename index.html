ALL YOU NEED TO KNOW

Your trial period is totally free! After that you can choose to pay monthly (€20), quarterly (€45) or yearly (€120). After your free trial ends, you'll be charged for your chosen membership. You'll receive an email 48 hours before payment is taken. Subscription renews automatically unless you cancel, and you can cancel anytime.

All you need is a 2 x 2m space and your phone (iPhone or Android). Ideally you need a fitness mat, especially for yoga and floor exercises. There are classes with resistance bands, dumbbells and kettlebells, but also hundreds of bodyweight classes if you don't have equipment. If you have an Apple Watch, MyZone, Polar or Wahoo heart rate tracker, you can see your stats live and progress.

600+ classes and 20+ training plans with world-class trainers, whenever you want. Connect with a fitness tracker (available in our shop or connect with your own bluetooth-enabled HR tracker) to see your live stats, track progress and connect to the leaderboard in group workouts. You can also join challenges to stay accountable and keep levelling up! (You don't need a fitness tracker to get started.)
